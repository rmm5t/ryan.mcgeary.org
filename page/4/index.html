Ryan McGeary is a software developer and consultant, business starter, rubyist (at heart), polyglot (in practice), web guru, father, CrossFit nerd, and former amateur triathlete and collegiate swimmer. Ryan owns McGeary Consulting Group and is the founder of BusyConf and ChargeStack. He lives near Boulder, Colorado.

Talk: CoffeeScript at 757rb Nov 1, 2010

NORFOLK, VIRGINIA — 757rb — I discovered CoffeeScript during a recent project that Jim Garvin and I were working on. We knew the app was going to be heavy on the JavaScript, and that’s how we started – writing straight JavaScript. Even though I have written JavaScript for years, this time, the JavaScript syntax itself got in the way. CoffeeScript’s elegant syntax relieved the burden.

I quickly fell so in love with CoffeeScript that I have since sworn off writing raw JavaScript directly ever again. The goal of this talk is to convince the audience to try CoffeeScript in hopes that they too will find it as valuable as I did.
